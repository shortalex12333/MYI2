## Key Topics We Cover

1. **Hull & Machinery Coverage** - Physical damage protection, collision, sinking, fire, theft, weather damage
2. **Protection & Indemnity (P&I)** - Liability for injury, property damage, pollution, wreck removal
3. **Agreed Value vs Actual Cash Value** - Policy valuation methods and total loss payouts
4. **Named Storm Deductibles** - Florida-specific percentage-based deductibles for hurricanes
5. **Navigation Limits & Lay-Up Warranties** - Geographic coverage areas and seasonal restrictions
6. **Charter & Commercial Use** - How chartering affects coverage and endorsement requirements
7. **Crew Medical & Jones Act** - Professional crew coverage, maintenance and cure, Jones Act liability
8. **Regional Requirements** - State-specific regulations and market conditions
